# Refund Policy — NectarPress

**Nectar Digit Pvt. Ltd.**  
**Effective date:** 2026-05-22

---

## 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee (New Purchases)

If you purchase a NectarPress license and are not satisfied within **14 days** of purchase, we will refund your full payment — no questions asked.

**How to request:**
1. Email billing@nectardigit.com within 14 days of purchase
2. Subject line: "Refund Request — [last 4 chars of your license key]"
3. We process refunds within 3 Nepal business days (Monday–Friday, 10 AM–6 PM NPT)

**Refund method:** Same channel as original payment (Khalti → Khalti, Bank → Bank transfer to your account).

**Note on Khalti refunds:** Khalti's refund processing time is typically 3–7 business days after we initiate it.

---

## Pro-Rated Refunds (Accidental Renewals)

If your license auto-renews and you did not intend to renew, contact us within **7 days** of the renewal charge for a pro-rated refund.

**Conditions:**
- Reported within 7 days of the renewal transaction
- License has not been activated on any domain since renewal
- Or license was activated but immediately deactivated on the same day

---

## No Refunds

Refunds are **not available** for:
- Purchases more than 14 days old (except accidental renewal — see above)
- Licenses that have been in active use and are more than 14 days old
- Evaluation-mode activations (free, no charge to refund)
- Purchases made by an authorized reseller or agency on behalf of a client

---

## License Cancellation (vs. Refund)

Cancelling a license is different from requesting a refund:
- **Cancel:** You stop the subscription from auto-renewing. Your license remains active until its current expiry date. No refund.
- **Refund:** You receive your payment back. The license is deactivated immediately.

To cancel auto-renewal: log in to [nectardigit.com/account](https://nectardigit.com/account) → License → Cancel Renewal.

---

## Enterprise Contracts

Enterprise-tier customers who signed a custom contract are subject to the refund terms in that contract, which may differ from this policy. If there is a conflict between this policy and your signed contract, the signed contract governs.
